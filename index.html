Scholastic Achievements

  • Madhav Manish Gulati, student Class XII, has made the school proud again. He has qualified Government of India’s prestigious “Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana (KVPY)” and secured a place for himself at IISc Bangalore as a KVPY fellow, the results declared in the month of August 2020. Earlier, having qualified in the Regional Mathematical Olympiad (RMO), appeared INMO (Indian National Mathematical Olympiad), NSEJS and NSEA qualifier, SMTE finalist (Attended the Mumbai Seminar) also an NTSE scholar (attended IIT Roorkee Nurturance Program) now has added another feather to his cap.

  • Paakhi Maheswari ranked amongst Top 11 – 500, at All India Level in Essay Competition(English), in the category of Classes XI & XII, of ISRO Cyberspace Competitions - 2020

  • Namrata Jha, a student of Class XII, 2020-21 batch has achieved the incredible feat of scoring 99.98 percentile in the JEE Main, 2021(February attempt). She has become the topper of not only Noida City, but the whole of U. P State, amongst girl candidates.


  • SPARK Innovation Awards by Dr. K V Rao Scientific Society: These coveted awards are given to the projects that display harnessing of scientific concepts and offering solutions to everyday issues through some novel concepts. 7 students from Classes VII-X participated in this event. Aayush Bhansali of Class X qualified the first round and has presented his project in the Final round on March 20, 2021.

  • Vidyarthi Vigyan Manthan: Kartikeye Dimri of Class X secured the Second position in the State level Camp, thereby qualifying for the National level of Vidyarthi Vigyan Manthan, 2 India’s largest Science Talent Search Digital examination. He was awarded a cash prize of Rs. 3000/- and a certificate.

  • INSPIRE AWARD: For the prestigious INSPIRE AWARD, one of the flagship programmes of the Department of Science & Technology, Government of India, four Science projects of Parv Jain (Class VII), Subhanshu Yadav (Class VIII), Kanav Sehgal (Class IX) and Kartikeya Dimri (Class X) were submitted as entries from DPS, Greater Noida. It is a matter of pride that all the 4 projects have been selected for the District level participation. Also, the students received a cash prize of Rs. 10,000/- each to develop their projects. The school is honing these projects for National Level participation.


  • Pratishtha Maan, a student of Class X (2020-21) has been selected for the prestigious Harvard Summer School Program, offered by the Harvard University, USA. She exhibited outstanding academic prowess and superlative application skills to secure her seat, surpassing an exceptional pool of applicants from across the globe.

  • IAPT - NSE: Amey Raj, Ekansh Singh, Madhav Manish Gulati, Tanisha Chandra and Vardan Jain of Class XII (2019-20) were amongst the 10% students who were awarded with certificates for their remarkable performance in the National Standard Examination organised by The Indian Association of Physics Teachers, on November 2019. The results were declared in January 2021.

  • eRaksha: In collaboration with NCERT and NCPCR, the Cyber Peace Foundation conducted eRaksha competition-2020 from June 30 to September 30, 2020 to ensure cyber safety of the young users and also to enable educators and caregivers to provide guidance to young minds and help them maximise opportunity and minimise the risk in online spaces. The theme for the competition was Digital Citizenship and Online Safety. The students were required to make a 10 minute video in Hindi or English language with proper captions. Aditya Sharma of Class VI won the Screen Masters Competition in the School Category.
